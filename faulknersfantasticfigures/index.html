Welcome to Faulkner's Fantastic Figures!
Here you'll find toys and model kits of epic proportions!

We offer a large variety of custom action figures, along with some more well-known characters from your favorite shows or video games. We also specialize in custom model kits, as well as popular kits ranging from Bandai’s Gundam series to Star Wars’ 1/12 scale series. While we offer services to design and create the custom figure of your dreams, we also provide the tools and supplies you need to customize the figure on your own. And if that’s not enough, we also provide 3D printing services so you can truly create the perfect figure to add to your collection.
