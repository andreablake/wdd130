Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Faulkner’s Fantastic Figures! Here you’ll find toys and model kits of epic proportions. We offer a large variety of custom action figures, along with some more well-known characters from your favorite shows or video games. We also specialize in custom model kits, as well as popular kits ranging from Bandai’s Gundam series to Star Wars’ 1/12 scale series. While we offer services to design and create the custom figure of your dreams, we also provide the tools and supplies you need to customize the figure on your own. And if that’s not enough, we also provide 3D printing services so you can truly create the perfect figure to add to your collection.

Audience

This website will provide fun for all ages through the building of model kits and other accessories. The intended audience is technically for everyone, as there will be figures and kits for all ages, but the main age group will be teenagers to adults. This is for all the creative types who still like to enjoy the fun of playing with toys and building something new. And to take it even further, those who want to design and create custom figures will find all the tools necessary to create their perfect dream figure. From paints to airbrushes, to even 3D printing, this site will have it all.

Figures and Model Kits

Take a look at our selection of figures and model kits from popular franchises, ranging from your favorite TV shows to video games, and even some book series. We offer collector’s boxes, special editions, and limited-edition vinyl figures, as well as pre-ordering on most items. Our figures and model kits are carefully selected from trusted toy brands to promise you the highest quality in our products. If we don’t have a figure or model kit you’re looking forward, we can order it for you straight from the source. Become a member with us to sign up for discounts, pre-order announcements, free shipping, and much more!

Images for the Page 2

3D Printing and Customization

If collecting figures or building model kits isn’t enough to satisfy your craving to create, you can go the extra mile and customize your figures and models with our premium paints and top of the line airbrushes. From a large array of colors and finishes, you can make your customized figure pop to life and off of the shelf. And if customizing the figures and model kits we offer still isn’t enough, we also have 3D printing services so you can send us your personal designs to print, finish, paint, and ship directly to your home. We offer a variety of materials to print with, from plastics to sandstone. Prices will vary with the size of the design being printed.
